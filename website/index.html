FAQ

Common Questions

Is my financial data safe? ▼
Yes. Each user's data is completely separate — stored in its own private file on our server, identified only by your Telegram ID. No one else can see your positions, strategies, or trading history.
How do payments work? No auto-charges? ▼
We never store your payment details. You pay manually each month via PayPal, Venmo, or Stripe. No automatic charges. When your subscription expires, access stops — no surprise bills ever.
What brokers does it support? ▼
The bot gives strategies you place on any US broker — Robinhood, Fidelity, TD Ameritrade, Schwab, etc. Send screenshots from any broker and it reads your trades automatically.
How do I cancel? ▼
Sign in to trade-oracle-ai.com with your access code, go to Subscription, and click Cancel. Or send /cancel to the bot on Telegram. Your access continues until the end of your paid period.
Do I need trading experience? ▼
No experience needed. The bot is designed for beginners. It explains every strategy in plain English, gives step-by-step instructions, and even tells you which exact buttons to tap on Robinhood or Fidelity.
How much money do I need to start? ▼
You can start with $1,000 for put credit spreads. For the full wheel strategy and income plan, $10,000–$25,000 is recommended. The bot adjusts its strategies to your account size automatically.
